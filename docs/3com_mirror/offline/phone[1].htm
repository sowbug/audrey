Phone Support Information
Need help loading drivers? Having difficulty with connections? Your 3Com Support Plan gives you access to toll-free telephone support 24 hours a day, seven days a week*. Simply call 1-866-326-6226, any time of the day, to speak with one of our highly trained engineers. Prior to speaking with an engineer, you will be asked to provide your Support ID, which can be found in the Welcome Kit email you received after activating your Support Plan. Upon entering your valid Support ID number, you will be transferred to an engineer for assistance.
