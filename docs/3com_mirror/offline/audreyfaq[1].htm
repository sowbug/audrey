General Audrey Question:

Q Does it hurt to leave Audrey on all the time?

    a No. In fact, it is a good idea because then Audrey is ready whenever you need to access the web, calendar, e-mail, and all the other functions available. It also ensures that updates to e-mail and channels are current.

Q Is there a way to back up Audrey?

    a If Audrey is synchronized to the Palm handheld compatible devices, then the datebook and address book will be backed up. Look for announcements in the near future regarding another way to back up Audrey. Go to http://www.3com.com/ergo to keep up on any new feature announcements.

Q How can the touch screen be cleaned?

    a Use monitor screen cleaning pads or a water dampened cloth.

Q Why does Audrey ask for credit card information?

    a Entering your credit card information is optional. In the future, Audrey will make online shopping easier by automatically providing this information to channel partners and certain websites when you're making purchases. This feature is not yet implemented, but will be soon.

Q When Audrey started the first time, the time zone had to be set. Why did the clock have to be set too?

    a Audrey doesn't detect your time zone. If you're not in EST (Eastern Standard Time), you'll need to adjust your time zone and clock. The next time Audrey updates the clock it will adjust the time and zone automatically.

Q Why does it seem like Audrey's volume is being reset or changed sometimes?

    a Under certain circumstances, Audrey will "warm boot". When this occurs, the volume may be reset to default levels. If this does occur, correct it by going into your Actions Palette, select Volume, and tap the Test button. This will restore your settings.

Q When I am traveling for long periods, can I unplug and store Audrey in the closet without losing all the configuration information such as POP3 and SMTP mail server names?

    a Yes, Audrey can be unplugged and stored for several months without losing the configuration information. That information is stored in the flash memory. Upon plugging Audrey in and powering up, you will have to update the clock, channels, and e-mail.

Q Why does Audrey's screen go blank?

    a Audrey "goes to sleep" when there is a period of inactivity. "Waking Audrey up" is done with a touch or tap to the screen.

Q I don't need Audrey to update channels and check for e-mail five times a day. Is there away to limit the number of times the modem dials out?

    a Yes. Press the Actions Palette button, tap Audrey options, tap Download Schedule, and erase the times listed that you don't want Audrey to call out. If they're blank, the modem won't dial out.

Q Why doesn't Audrey always wake up when I push the power button?

    a Try waking Audrey up via alternate means; for example, try tapping the screen or pressing any key on the keyboard.

Q Why was the name Audrey chosen for this Internet Appliance Device?

    a In selecting a name for 3Com's new Internet Appliance Device, the company sought to give its new product a personality with an approachable, non-technical name since it would be integrated into the home as if it were a member of the family. 3Com selected the name Audrey from a long list of names because 3Com felt that the name Audrey suggests elegance and intellect. 3Com designed this particular Internet Appliance Device with a very elegant and pleasant design while having smart and focused software. 3Com felt that the name Audrey reflected those characteristics.

Date Book Questions:

Q Why can't I have a datebook application other than the one on Audrey?

    a Audrey can support up to three (two palm devices and one Audrey) date books, but they have to originate from a device using the Palm Operating System. There will be an Add option in the near future to use other datebook applications.

E-mail Questions:

Q Why can't I use Scribble or Voice when replying to a message?

    a Audrey supports replying with text messages only.

Q Why can't I view certain e-mails from mailing lists, specifically HTML e-mail.

    a Audrey doesn't currently support HTML formatted e-mail. This will be addressed with a software update in the near future.

Q Why can't the voice e-mail I sent from Audrey to a Macintosh computer be played?

    a The version of Quicktime that by default plays WAV files on a Macintosh doesn't support the specific file format we're using. Check into a third-party application for the Mac to play WAV files (such as SoundApp).

Q When listening to audio with RealPlayer, why don't the fast-forward and rewind buttons work?

    a These buttons are not currently implemented.

Q Can everyone in the family have their own e-mail box on Audrey?

    a No, Audrey only has one e-mail box. This is a family communication and organization device rather than a computer. Therefore, to make its use simple there is only one mailbox. If your ISP offers more e-mail boxes, these extra e-mail boxes can be used on your PC system.

Q Does Audrey support MSN e-mail?

    a No, at this time Audrey doesn't support MSN e-mail.

Q Will Audrey support Yahoo mail accounts?

    a Yes. Since Yahoo mail can be accessed through standard mail servers, Audrey can handle this.

Q How long of a voice message can I record with Audrey?

    a Audrey can record voice messages up to three minutes. These messages are about 400K in size when sent.

Q How much e-mail can Audrey store?

    a Audrey can store up to one (1) MB of incoming mail, and half (1/2) MB in the Send later folder.

Web Browser Questions:

Q While browsing the web or channels, sometimes a "web browser out of memory," error appears. Why?

    a Since Audrey has limited resources, some web pages will cause this problem. Web pages with non-standard Java Script, pages with large animated GIF's or images, and browsing many pages containing Flash animation can cause this.

    If you run into this condition, go to another application (such as the datebook or e-mail) and then go back to try the browser again. If this doesn't eliminate the error message, power Audrey down for a few seconds and then power it back up. This will clear any extraneous data that remains in memory. Channels will need to be updated again.

Q Can I open the Actions Palette while listening to RealAudio?

    a No, close RealPlayer and then open Actions Palette.

Q On certain web pages, a message stating, "you need to upgrade your browser" appears. How do I do this?

    a These websites are using HTML code that can't be handled by Netscape 3.0. Audrey's browser is compatible with Netscape 3, but unlike Netscape 3 it cannot be upgraded. This may be changed in a future software update.

ISP and Broadband Questions:

Q Why doesn't Audrey dial the entire phone number for the Internet service provider?

    a In Internet Setup, make sure that the entire phone number for the ISP is entered, including area code. If the area code isn't entered, Audrey may incorrectly dial telephone numbers.

Q Why does Audrey appear to set up an account with a different ISP when I use the keyboard to select "Custom ISP?"

    a Instead of using the keyboard to select custom, select it with the touch screen icon.

Q Why does switching modes affect the performance of Audrey? If the broadband connection is unavailable, I use the modem, then switch back to broadband.

    a Due to the number of changes made to Audrey when switching from one mode to another, we recommend powering Audrey down for a few seconds, and then powering back up when switching from a broadband connect to a modem, or visa versa.

Q My phone company forces me to dial my area code even to make local calls. Can Audrey do this?

    a Yes. In the Actions Palette, under Internet Settings in the ISP Dialing section, uncheck the "Local Call" box.

    If you don't have to dial a 1 first, simply enter your area code in the Dialing Prefix box, instead of not checking Local Call.

Q Why won't Audrey dial out when I have voice mail message on my phone company message service?

    a The modem on Audrey can't detect a steady dial tone when you have messages. To resolve this, go into the Actions Palette, select Internet Settings, and enter ATX3 in the "Extra Settings" box of the ISP dialing section. Then the modem will dial regardless of whether or not there's a dial tone on the line.

Q Can I use Audrey with my AOL account?

    a No. For a list of compatible ISPs, visit http://www.3com.com/ergo

Q Can I use Audrey on my Cable or DSL broadband service?

    a Yes, in most circumstances, Audrey will support cable and DSL connections that don't require PPPoE. If your provider uses PPPoE, check into getting a third-party network HUB that can take care of the PPPoE authentication. Then Audrey will work with your broadband provider as well.

Channel Questions:

Q Why don't all the selected channels display sometimes? I turn the channel dial and get a blank screen or message "This channel is not currently available."

    a A channel will not display if an update failed. This can happen if the download is cancelled while the channel is updating or the server containing the channel update information is down. You can always manually update the channel with the "Update Now" icon on the channel screen. You can update all channels by pressing the Action Palette button, tapping Audrey options, Download Schedule, and Update all channels now.

Q Why do I sometimes get a blank page when I click a link in the ABCNews channel to read a story?

    a This will be addressed in a software update. Select a different story and then go back to the story you originally tried to read.

Q How can the channels always be on if Audrey is not connected continually to the Internet?

    a The channels are specially formatted to deliver information in a cached environment. Content is downloaded to Audrey frequently so the information reflects the latest information available. Downloads are automatic and require no action by you. You can request to have a channel updated at a time other than the scheduled downloads with the "Update Now" icon.

Q How do I know what channels are available and how do I set them?

    a A list of current channels is available at http://www.3com.com/Ergo and in the Actions Palette. To see a channel, open the Actions Palette, and tap the Channel Select icon.

Printing Questions:

Q What USB printers does Audrey support?

    a Currently, Audrey supports three Canon USB Printers: BJC85, BJC85W and the BJC2100.

Q Why do I get a blank page when I attempt to print a channel?

    a Before printing, tap the center of the screen over the story you wish to print. This will give the area containing your story "focus" so Audrey knows what you want to print.

Q Why do I get only the first page when I print a story from a channel?

    a Most of the channels use "framed" web pages. When printing from a framed page, you'll only get the contents of the current frame. In order to print complete stories, it's recommended that you print from the channel's normal website (for example www.abcnews.com).

Q Why won't Audrey print at all after the printer was accidentally unplugged while it was printing?

    a If you run into this, simply unplug Audrey for a few seconds, plug Audrey back in, and power on.

Q Is it possible to print the entire address book?

    a Currently no, this is not possible. In certain circumstances, Audrey can only print information that fits on the screen, or a "screen capture." If your address book is synchronized with a Palm device, you can print the address book from a PC.

Q Is the color printing slow?

    a Audrey's printer is rated at an average speed of two pages per minute when printing in color. Very full pages or large images, however can take over two minutes to print a single page. If speedier printouts are required, we recommend temporarily switching to black and white.

Q When I print images in color, why do they appear to be low quality?

    a Even though the printer is capable of printing 720 dots per inch (DPI) in color mode, currently Audrey is limited to 360 DPI. This will be changed in a future software update.

Palm Compatible Device Questions:

Q What Palm devices does Audrey support?

    a Audrey supports all Palm compatible organizers, and supports Handspring Visor organizers using either serial or USB cradles. These devices all use the Palm Operating System.

Q Can I synchronize my Palm device through Audrey's infrared port instead of attaching the cradle to the serial port or the USB port?

    a No, you can't. Audrey's infrared port can only be used for the keyboard included with Audrey.

Q How much Palm data can I store on Audrey?

    a Since the size of data varies, we can only provide approximations. Audrey can store approximately 2000 contacts, and 4000 events per Palm device user.

Q How many Palm devices can I synchronize to Audrey?

    a Audrey will support two devices using Palm Operating System, in addition to the internal date book.

Q Why are changes not reflected after a HotSync®? I added and removed some records from my Palm (or Audrey).

    a Sometimes when records are added and removed from the Palm (or Audrey) they are not reflected after a HotSync. If you HotSync again, these changes should be reflected.

Q Why did the HotSync with a Palm device abort?

    a Certain error conditions will cause this to happen on rare occasions. Try the HotSync again.
