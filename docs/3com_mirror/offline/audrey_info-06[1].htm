I need a new dial-up Internet access account with AT&T WorldNet service.

Follow the directions and fill out the screens for signing up with this service. Billing information is requested and credit card information needs to be provided. Upon submission of the billing information form, you will be provided with the login information and an opportunity to change any of that information. The required acceptance of the service agreement screen follows and upon tapping the continue button, a selection of numbers to access the ISP will appear. Select the telephone number nearest your location and tap continue. Tap continue on the "Congratulations" screen, and Audrey will connect to your new ISP.

    NOTE: Audrey can only support one e-mail user ID. The other five can be used on your computer system.
