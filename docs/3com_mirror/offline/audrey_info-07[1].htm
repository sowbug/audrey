Audrey Connected

The first time Audrey connects successfully to your ISP, the "set date and time" screen will appear. Audrey will attempt to download the correct time and date from the Internet. Make any necessary changes to this screen such as time, time zone, or date.

The next screen displays a dialog box with the message "Updating Channels, please wait." Allow the update of the channels. Channels are specially formatted for Audrey web content templates that are always available even when Audrey is not connected to the Internet. This first download updating channels will take a little longer than subsequent downloads because all the channel setup information and templates are being downloaded and updated.

    NOTE: Audrey is configured for five daily downloads (analog connection) or hourly downloads (broadband connection). These downloads update the channels and download e-mail.

Audrey can be left on at all times. The screen will go into a "sleep" mode after a period of inactivity. For more information on Channels, click here. For more information on Downloads, click here.
