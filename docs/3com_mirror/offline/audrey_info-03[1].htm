I already have a dial-up (analog modem) Internet access account

Audrey contains a 56K analog modem to connect to your ISP. Connect Audrey to your telephone wall jack with the RJ11 cable. There are two RJ11 ports located on the back of Audrey. One can be used to connect to the wall jack and the other to connect to another analog device such as a phone. Audrey should be directly connected to the wall jack with nothing in between.

    NOTE: The phone line can be plugged into either RJ11 port on Audrey.

If your ISP is listed as a friendly or preferred provider on Audrey, then all you need is the:

  • telephone number to access your ISP via modem
  • your e-mail address
  • your e-mail password

Obtain the following information for configuring Audrey if you chose a custom ISP:

    NOTE: The information can be obtained from the ISP or from the TCP/IP settings screen of the computer** used to access your ISP.

  • telephone number to access your ISP via modem
  • incoming mail server name (POP3)
  • outgoing mail server name (SMTP)

If your ISP assigns static IP addresses, you need to obtain the following information:

  • your assigned IP address
  • your ISP subnet mask
  • your ISP gateway
  • primary DNS setting
  • secondary DNS setting

Remember to add the necessary prefixes for dialing out to your ISP. Tap the 9 option, if you need to dial a 9 to access an outside line. If your telephone service includes call waiting, you can turn off the call waiting feature while connected with Audrey by tapping the *70 option.

**How to get the information from Microsoft Outlook and Netscape Communicator regarding the POP3 and SMTP mail server names on PCs and Macintosh computers:

  Find Mail Server Names 1

PC     Microsoft Outlook 5.0

PC     Netscape Communicator 4

Open the Microsoft Outlook application on your PC, select the Tools menu, and click Accounts. Click the Mail tab, highlight which account you wish to check, and click Properties. On the Servers tab, check the mail server names to get the information needed for Audrey.

Open the Netscape Communicator application on your PC, select the Edit menu, and click Preferences. Click the plus sign (+) next to the Mail & Newsgroup category and click Mail Server. Check the Outgoing mail (SMTP) server: name and the Incoming mail server: name to get the information needed for Audrey.

Macintosh     Microsoft Outlook 5.0

Macintosh     Netscape Communicator 4

Open the Outlook application on your Macintosh, select the Tools menu, and click Accounts. Click the Mail tab, highlight which account (e-mail) you wish to check, and click Edit on top bar. Check the mail server names to get the information needed for Audrey.

Open the Netscape Communicator application on your Macintosh, select the Edit menu, and click Preferences. Click Mail Servers under the Mail & Newsgroup category. Check the Outgoing mail (SMTP) server: name and the Incoming mail server: name to get the information needed for Audrey.
