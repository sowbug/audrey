Channels

Audrey provides the ability to have 12 pages of Internet content channeled directly to Audrey's screen and available by turning the channel knob. Six are referred to as core channels and their paths and images depicting the channels are tailored and formatted in Audrey at the factory; they cannot be replaced with other channels. Those six core channels are:

ABCNews

Comprehensive coverage of international and national news.

ESPN

Up-to-date coverage and scores for major sporting events.

Mr. Showbiz

Reviews of movies, music, and television.

CBS MarketWatch

World financial news and market results. Get real-time quotes and track your favorite stock.

Digital City

Information about the city where you live or visit. Local entertainment, restaurants, and shopping.

AccuWeather

The latest weather information for your locality including extended weather forecasts and the ability to check weather anywhere in the world.

The next three channels are default selections. You may deselect or replace any or all of these default channels at the "Channel Select" panel.

Drugstore.com

Obtain information, get advice, and shop for prescriptions and over-the-counter drugs.

Food.com

Order takeout or delivery from neighborhood restaurants.

Cyberbills

View, manage, and pay your bills.

The last three channels can be selected from the "Channel Select" panel. As new channels become available to Audrey, they will be added to the "Channel Select" panel at the next code update. The list of available channels can be found at http://www.3com.com/ergo also. When a channel is selected, no image depicting that channel will be available when turning the channel knob. After the channel is updated with a normally scheduled update or a manual update using the download icon on the download screen, a logo will appear for that channel on the dial selector. Only the core and default channels will have images on the dial selector.

If the channel provider only updates the channel twice a week or daily, there will not be a change to the channel at every download. Audrey will reflect the changes in channels updated hourly by their providers.

    NOTE: If Audrey has a broadband (cable or DSL) connection, the updates to the channels can be scheduled hourly or every 30 minutes. The default is hourly. If Audrey has an analog connection, the updates can be schedule once to five times a day. The default is five times at approximately 5-hour intervals.

    Some of the images and text on the channel page open the web browser and go to a web page. Those images will display a web browser icon and the text will be underlined. When tapped, Audrey will dial up the ISP, connect, and go to the web page. If a web page has been accessed unintentionally, return to the channel by depressing the channel knob. Audrey will disconnect automatically from the web page after fifteen minutes of inactivity. A manual termination of the connection can be made by pressing the Actions palette button and tapping the disconnect icon in the lower left corner of the screen.

    NOTE: If Audrey has a broadband (cable or DSL) connection, do not tap the disconnect button on the action palette screen. Broadband connections are always connected.

Some channels have a "Channel Preference" screen to customize the channel. While in the Channel application, touch the Action Palette button and tap the Channel Preference icon. Those channels that offer preferences are:

  • AccuWeather: add two more cities in addition to your local area to display weather forecasts.
  • CBS MarketWatch: list 10 stocks to be monitored and displayed.
  • Digital City: choose a city from the list to show local entertainment, restaurants, and shopping information.

As mentioned in the section about turning Audrey on for the first time, the user information entered for address with town and postal code will customize the weather channel to display weather forecasts for that area.
