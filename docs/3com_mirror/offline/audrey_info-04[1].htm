I already have a broadband (cable or DSL) Internet access account

Audrey has two USB ports on the back (bottom of the cable housing): one for connecting to a broadband modem -- ADSL or Cable -- and the other can be used for attaching a printer.

There are the only two adapters that Audrey recognizes because the driver software is built-in.

  • An USB Ethernet Connect Kit (3C8351OP-01) is the recommended adapter.
  • The 3Com HomeConnect Home Network Ethernet adapter, 10Mbps USB interface (3C460) is also compatible.

When selecting broadband access, Audrey will remind you to connect your 3Com USB Ethernet adapter to the USB port.

  • Plug one end of your Ethernet cable into an available Ethernet jack on your broadband device and the other end into the 3Com USB Ethernet adapter's Ethernet jack.
  • Plug one end of the USB cable into the USB jack on the adapter and the other end into one of Audrey's two USB ports.

Do you have a DHCP or an assigned/static IP account? Tap the icon that applies.

If the ISP assigns static IP addresses, obtain the following information from your ISP or from the TCP/IP settings screen of the computer used to access your ISP:

  • your assigned IP address
  • your ISP subnet mask
  • your ISP gateway
  • primary DNS setting
  • secondary DNS setting

The following information is also needed by Audrey:

  • ISP Host name (if required)
  • incoming mail server name (POP3)
  • outgoing mail server name (SMTP)
